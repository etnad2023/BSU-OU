FEATURES AND BENEFITS of Online Education

FEATURES AND BENEFITS of ONLINE EDUCATION

UBIQUITOUS LEARNING
Online resources for self-learning, including lectures that have been recorded, references, modules, and tests, are available at any time and from any location.

SELF PACED LEARNING
Online courses encourage learning at your own speed. The student can access the materials whenever they want, as long as it doesn’t conflict with their other obligations in life, school, or work.

TIME FLEXIBILITY AND ACADEMIC FREEDOM
You can study when it’s convenient for you and complete coursework while earning your degree online. Online courses give some people the chance to advance their education while maintaining their current employment.

STUDENT SUPPORT / LIFE COACHES
Students who study entirely online are given access to comprehensive student services. Students who need assistance with their academics or student life might turn to life coaches. They serve as advisors to students on issues pertaining to their totally online studies, including academic advice and enrolling, career counseling and placement options, goal-setting, time management and social skills coaching, as well as other interactions with the University.

REDUCED COSTS
Online education can cost less, as it eliminates some miscellaneous fees and other school-associated costs such as transportation or fuel, board and lodging, etc.
